Instructions

Your input variable will contain a fibonacci sequence number. Your job is to calculate the next n digits.

Inputs

value1
The starting number

value2
n number to calculate after value1

Sample Test Cases

Small Number
Value 1: 5
Value 2: 2
Output: 8,13
Large Numbers
Value 1: 34
Value 2: 7
Output: 55,89,144,233,377,610,987
